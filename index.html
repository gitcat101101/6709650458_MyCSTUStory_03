About Me

Hi! My name is Paponpat Meevon or you can call me Pokpong, and I am currently a first-year student at Thammasat University, majoring in Compurter Science. Although I am just beginning my academic journey, I have already developed a strong interest in the fields of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Data Science. While I have not yet had the opportunity to study these subjects in depth, I am eager to learn more as I progress through my studies. I believe that these technologies will play a crucial role in shaping the future, and I am excited to be part of that journey.
