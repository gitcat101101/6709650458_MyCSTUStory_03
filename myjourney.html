Year 1

The first year was all about exploring the basics of Computer Science. I learned foundational concepts like programming, algorithms, and data structures. It was challenging, but it sparked my interest in the field.

Year 2

In the second year, I started diving deeper into more complex topics like databases, networking, and operating systems. I also began working on small projects that helped me apply my knowledge in practical ways.

Year 3

The third year was crucial in developing my technical skills. I took courses in software development, artificial intelligence, and machine learning. This year, I also worked on a major project that prepared me for real-world challenges.

Year 4

My final year was both exciting and rewarding. I completed my capstone project, which involved building a full-stack application. After four years of hard work and dedication, I finally graduated with a degree in Computer Science. This moment marks the beginning of my journey into the tech industry.
